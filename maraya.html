Most Challenging or Favorite Lesson

The complexity of building a website was by far the most difficult thing I had to learn in this course. When I found faults and mistakes in my code, the procedure turned into a difficult journey. To overcome these obstacles, one needed a combination of persistence, analytical thinking, and a thorough understanding of coding and debugging. Even though I was first frustrated, getting past these obstacles was a valuable learning experience that helped me better grasp web programming. Conversely, the moment my website was finally finished, marking the end of my endeavor, was my favorite aspect of the course. It was really satisfying to witness a finished, well-functioning product. It gave me a concrete example of my developing abilities in addition to validating the effort I put into conquering obstacles.
